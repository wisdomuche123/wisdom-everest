Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about website maintenance

Why do I need website maintenance?

Regular maintenance ensures your website remains secure, functional, and optimized for performance. It prevents security breaches, maintains compatibility, and provides better user experience.

How often should my website be updated?

We recommend weekly updates for security patches and monthly updates for features and performance improvements. The frequency depends on your website's complexity and traffic.

What happens if my website gets hacked?

With our maintenance plans, we provide immediate response to security incidents, malware removal, and restoration from clean backups to get your site back online quickly.

Can I cancel my maintenance plan anytime?

Yes, our maintenance plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice.
