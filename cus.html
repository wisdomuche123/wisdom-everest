Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our custom web design services

How long does a custom website take to build?

Most custom website projects take between 4-8 weeks from initial consultation to launch, depending on complexity and scope. We provide a detailed timeline during the discovery phase.

Do you provide website hosting?

Yes, we offer reliable hosting solutions optimized for your website's performance and security. We can also help you migrate to your preferred hosting provider if needed.

Can you redesign my existing website?

Absolutely! We specialize in website redesigns that improve both aesthetics and functionality while preserving your existing content and SEO value.

Do you provide training for managing the website?

Yes, we provide comprehensive training and documentation to ensure you can confidently manage your website content after launch.
